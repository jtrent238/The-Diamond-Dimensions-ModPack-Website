Diamond Dimensions Modded Survival

Welcome to The Diamond Dimensions Modpack! 

This modpack is the official pack of the YouTube Channel, The Diamond Minecart where a survival series of this pack was first played. Everything you see in the series is found within this pack and you can now play it for yourself! The theme behind the modpack is exploring different dimensions and using the items you can find there to enhance your normal survival experience. Find magical weapons, explore different dimensions and take down insane mobs in this awesome pack!
